Since 1979

In 1979, Al Wadi Al Akhdar was established with 3 core products: Hummus Tahina, Foul Moudammas & Chickpeas. Today more than 200 products are the number one choice of households across Lebanon and the world.

Born out of a love for our culinary heritage, we have established ourselves as experts in the Lebanese table. Quality and consistency are the keystones of our success.
