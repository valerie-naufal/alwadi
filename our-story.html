Our Story

It all started in 1979,
at the time, Lebanon was at war, and the food supply was running low. That is when Bechara Obegi and Frank Farage founded Al Wadi Al Akhdar – a name that speaks the language of the nation, and a brand the people can trust.
The two partners started by using the finest ingredients to make the three staples of the Lebanese Cuisine: Hummus Tahina, Foul Moudammas, and Chickpeas. With great reception by the market, innovation, and dedication, Al Wadi Al Akhdar kept expanding to offer today more than 200 unique products belonging to over 15 different food categories.
Al Wadi Al Akhdar’s exceptional flavor and distinctive touch make it the number one choice of households across Lebanon and other parts of the world.
Because at the heart of every Lebanese family gathering, friends’ reunion, and celebration, there’s Al Wadi Al Akhdar.

1980's

In 1979, Al Wadi was established with three core products: Hummus Tahina, Foul Moudammas, and Chickpeas.

In 1984, the Dove Processing factory fully began to manufacture our Middle Eastern Specialty. The canned vegetable category was launched. First export to the USA and France.

Late 80's

Baba Ghannouge done the traditional way was launched.

Al Wadi was the only brand to roast the eggplants, others grill.

Four new Jams were launched in the market, shortly followed by Tahina.

1990's

In 1992, Al Wadi became a market leader in Lebanese Specialties.

The first brand to launch frozen vegetables in Lebanon.

In 1997, Al Wadi became a market leader in canned vegetables.

2000's

In 2004, Al Wadi became a market leader in Frozen Vegetables.

Between 2005 and 2010, our turnover in Lebanon doubled to reach 14M$.

2013

1st brand in Lebanon to establish a presence on social media.

Our website won the Golden award under the food category in the Lebanese and Arab region Pan Arab Web Awards.

2014

Our Pomegranate Molasses received the Bon Appetit (US food magazine) seal of approval.

A range of All Natural products: Waters and Pomegranate Molasses were launched to satisfy consumer needs worldwide.

TODAY

We opened our laboratory of innovation to meet the growing demand for research development.

We began distributing in Morocco, Jordan, and the GCC.

Our products are being exported to more than 30 countries.

Our goal: To be the leader in Lebanese Specialties.

We launched our organic range
