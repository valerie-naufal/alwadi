Our Commitment

Al Wadi is a trusted Lebanese food expert with a long legacy in the Lebanese market. Today, our name has become synonymous with Lebanese cuisine.

Born out of a love for our culinary heritage, we have established ourselves as experts in the Lebanese table.

Our products are prepared using the finest ingredients, the highest food standards, and the best and latest technologies to ensure top quality and safety.

As a fierce leader in the Lebanese market, we aim to gain equity strength and more recognition within the food industry regionally and internationally.

Leading in quality

Quality and consistency are the cornerstones of Al Wadi Al Akhdar’s success. We follow the highest standards across every single product we buy. We prepare our food with love and care, just as you would at home. We combine the latest food technology with the traditional secrets that go into making superior and exquisite food preparations for you to enjoy, again and again.
